WHY METAVEGAS ?





PROFIT

When the House Loses, $MXP Tokens are removed from the Liquidity Pool as payout to the winners. The team does not pay from team holdings

In turn, the $MXP value of EACH LP Token decreases

LP TOKENS

When the House wins, the Winnings are deposited into the Liquidity Pool and the House does NOT receive LP Tokens.

In turn, the $MXP value of EACH LP Token rises.

DAO

Being decentralized, the Community provides the $MXP tokens that the games operate on. By providing $MXP Tokens to a Liquidity Pool, players receive back LP Tokens which represent your fractional share of the Liquidity Pool and voting power in DAO goveranance proposals.
